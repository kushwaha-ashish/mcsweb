Our Mission

HERE WILL BE A PICTURE, CHILD BEING HELPED BY A TEACHER

We aim to to provide an environment to the children where their intellectual, emotional, physical and social needs are met. So, they can fulfill the goals they set for themselves.

Our Vision

HERE WILL BE A PICTURE, CHILD BEING HELPED BY A TEACHER

Our Vision is to have independent children who are strong support to their community, society and family.

Our goals are this this this

  • Develop self-confidence
  • Buiid a habit of concentration
  • Foster abiding curiosity
