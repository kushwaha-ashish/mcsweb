Outdoor Facility

Our half-acre playground consists of a huge running space, a snack area, a turf field, a woodchip area with a sandbox, swings, a climbing dome and a wood spiral tower. The open space is enclosed by a fence for children’s safety. Children also grow fruits and vegetables in the gardening boxes.
